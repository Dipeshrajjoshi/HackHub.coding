The Story of HACKHUB-CODE

LeetCode, HackerRank, and GitHub provide different types of challenges, but they are separate from each other. HACKHUB-CODE brings these platforms together. Our goal is to give organizers a central place to run contests. They can use problems from LeetCode, HackerRank, Codeforces, and GitHub repositories all in one place for participants.

Merged Ecosystem

Why limit a contest to just one site? With HACKHUB-CODE, an organizer can pick one problem from LeetCode, another from HackerRank, and a third from a specific GitHub repo. Participants no longer need to navigate multiple platforms; they find everything they need in a single, unified interface.

Manual Governance

We don't believe in rigid, automated grading that penalizes a missing semicolon. Our platform empowers human judges to evaluate logic, code architecture, and problem-solving creativity, providing participants with nuanced, professional feedback.
